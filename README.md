# The Unmoored Generation

**A Catholic Response to the Crisis of Purpose**

An initiative of the [Likoudis Legacy Foundation](https://likoudislegacy.org) bringing the Catholic intellectual tradition to bear on the crisis of meaning afflicting young adults.

## About

The Unmoored Generation is a data-backed, publicly accessible resource that:

- **Documents the crisis** with peer-reviewed data from the CDC, Pew Research Center, the Survey Center on American Life, and the research of Anne Case & Angus Deaton
- **Offers an anthropological diagnosis** rooted in the Catholic intellectual tradition — identifying the collapse of four pillars (telos, suffering, communion, transcendence) and recovering the patristic concept of *acedia*
- **Proposes concrete solutions** including the recovery of vocational language, rules of life, embodied community, and public intellectual engagement
- **Invites collaboration** from scholars, educators, pastors, and Catholic leaders

## Data Sources

All statistics are sourced and cited with clickable references to:

- CDC Youth Risk Behavior Survey (YRBS), 2013–2023
- Pew Research Center Religious Landscape Study, 2023–24
- Survey Center on American Life / American Enterprise Institute
- Case, A. & Deaton, A., *Deaths of Despair and the Future of Capitalism* (Princeton, 2020)
